Shipping & Delivery

Bringing authentic opulence directly to your doorstep.

At Sorah Salwar, we take pride in delivering highly curated, premium ethnic wear sourced authentically from Pakistan. Because we prioritize uncompromised quality and careful handling of our delicate hand-crafted pieces, our shipping process is deliberately meticulous.

Estimated Delivery Time

To ensure that every intricately designed outfit reaches you in absolute perfection, please allow 10 to 15 business days for delivery. This duration allows us to properly inspect, package, and safely dispatch your luxurious items internationally without rushing the care your outfit deserves.

Order Tracking

Once your order is processed and dispatched, you will receive a confirmation message containing your tracking details. You can monitor your shipment's journey at any time using our Track Order page.

Our Promise

While the craftsmanship and authentic sourcing take a little extra time, we assure you that the elegance and breathtaking quality of your Sorah Salwar pieces will make the wait entirely worthwhile!
