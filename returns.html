Returns & Exchanges

Our commitment to your satisfaction.

At Sorah Salwar, each piece is designed to bring elegance and comfort to your wardrobe. If something isn't quite right, we're here to make it easy for you.

Eligibility

Items must be unused, unworn, and in original condition with all tags intact. Requests should be made within 7 days of delivery.

How to Request

  • Go to My Orders
  • Select your item
  • Click Return/Exchange and submit your request

Exchanges

We offer exchanges for size or style, subject to availability.

Returns & Refunds

Once approved, we will guide you through the return process. Refunds will be issued to the original payment method within 5–7 working days after inspection.

Please Note

  • Sale or discounted items may not be eligible for return/exchange.
  • Shipping charges are non-refundable.
  • Any damaged or incorrect item must be reported within 24 hours of delivery.

For any assistance, our team is always happy to help.
